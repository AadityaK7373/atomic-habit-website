About the Book

Atomic Habits by James Clear is a powerful guide to creating positive change through tiny, consistent improvements.

Built on research from psychology and neuroscience, the book introduces the Four Laws of Behavior Change: Make it Obvious, Make it Attractive, Make it Easy, and Make it Satisfying.

Rather than focusing on goals, Clear emphasizes building systems — small daily actions that compound into meaningful results over time.

The book is filled with relatable stories, science-backed strategies, and actionable steps you can apply to any area of life.

Whether you want to eat healthier, focus better, or stay motivated, Atomic Habits gives you the tools to succeed.

A global bestseller with over 10 million copies sold, this book has helped millions transform their habits — and their lives.

Meet the Author

James Clear is a world-renowned expert on habit formation, decision-making, and personal growth.

He’s the author of Atomic Habits, a #1 New York Times bestseller that has sold over 10 million copies globally.

His work blends behavioral science with real-world application, helping people build systems for lasting success.

Clear’s simple, relatable approach makes habit-building accessible to anyone — from athletes and CEOs to students and creators.

He writes a popular weekly newsletter with over a million subscribers, sharing practical insights on performance, productivity, and mindset.

James has been featured in Time, Forbes, The Wall Street Journal, and more, and he speaks at top universities and global organizations.

His philosophy is simple: small changes make a big difference.

Through his writing and talks, James Clear continues to inspire people to take action, stay consistent, and become who they truly want to be.
